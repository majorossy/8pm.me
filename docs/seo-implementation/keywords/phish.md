## Quality Indicators & Terminology

### Recording Quality (Use in Metadata)
- `soundboard recording` - Highest quality, direct from console
- `audience recording` - Microphones in venue
- `matrix recording` - Blend of both
- `sbd` / `aud` - Abbreviations (used by tapers)

### Audio Format Keywords
- `flac` - Lossless audio codec (preferred by audiophiles)
- `lossless` - Generic high-quality term
- `24-bit` - Studio-quality recordings
- `mp3` - Compressed format (avoid emphasizing)

### Taper Community Terms
- `taper section` - Designated recording area
- `official release` - LivePhish.com releases
- `aud matrix` - Audience + soundboard blend
- `stealth recording` - Hidden microphone setup (avoid using)
